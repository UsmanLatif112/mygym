Rules & Regulations

Membership is subject to approval by the management committee. Membership card must be presented upon entering the facilities.

  • Smoking, alcoholic drinks and drugs are strictly prohibited.
  • Abusive language or uncivilized behaviour is not allowed.
  • All disputes to be decided by club management and its decision will be final and acceptable to all the members.
  • Club property not to be removed from the premises without the written permission of the management.
  • Any damage caused to the club property to be paid at the replacement cost decided by the management.
  • All personal belongings brought to the premises are at the owner's risk who will be responsible for their safe keeping.
  • Club management reserves the right to cancel any membership without giving any reason.
  • Members are required to deposit dues within three days of expiry date. Thereafter new admission fee will be charged.
  • All exercises are done at members' own risk. Management is not liable for any injury caused without instruction.
  • Members are advised to get doctor clearance before starting any exercise program. MY GYM management is not liable for any change in condition of health.
  • All dues paid are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • No allowance will be made in lieu of absence.
  • Exercise in shalwar qameez is not allowed.
Note: Management reserves the right to add, amend or delete any of the rules without prior notice.
